Festival Events

Experience the Best of Culture and Entertainment

Main Events

01

Music Concert

Experience an unforgettable evening of music featuring live performances by Vineeth Sreenivasan and other renowned artists. From soulful melodies to energetic beats, this concert promises to be the highlight of AAROHAN.

02

Pro Shows

Witness spectacular professional performances that showcase the pinnacle of artistic excellence. Celebrity guest appearances and world-class acts will leave you mesmerized and inspired.

03

Classical & Contemporary Dance

A beautiful fusion of traditional Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and modern contemporary dance styles. Watch as talented dancers tell stories through graceful movements and expressive choreography.

04

Fashion Show

The runway comes alive with stunning collections that blend traditional Indian attire with contemporary fashion. Witness designs that celebrate culture while embracing modern aesthetics.

05

Drama & Theatre

Immerse yourself in powerful theatrical performances that explore timeless themes and contemporary issues through compelling storytelling and exceptional acting.

06

Cultural Competitions

Participate in or cheer for talented individuals competing in various categories including solo singing, group dance, poetry recitation, and fine arts competitions.

Side Events

01

Car Show

Automobile enthusiasts rejoice! Explore an impressive collection of luxury cars, vintage classics, and modified beauties. Meet fellow car lovers and admire engineering excellence.

02

Fun Games

Engage in exciting recreational activities and interactive games suitable for all ages. From traditional games to modern challenges, fun is guaranteed for everyone.

03

Open Mic

Take the stage and share your talent! Whether you're a singer, poet, comedian, or storyteller, the open mic welcomes all performers to express themselves freely.

04

Art Exhibitions

Browse through curated art galleries featuring paintings, sculptures, photography, and digital art by both established and emerging artists.

05

Food Festival

Embark on a culinary journey with diverse food stalls offering regional delicacies, international cuisines, and innovative fusion dishes prepared by talented chefs.

06

Craft Bazaar

Discover handcrafted treasures from skilled artisans. From traditional handicrafts to modern artisan goods, find unique pieces to take home as memories.
